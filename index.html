Breakout Game!

How to play

The game begins with 1 row of bricks. Using a single ball, you must knock down all the bricks by using the walls and/or the paddle below to hit the ball against the bricks and eliminate them in order to go to the next level. Level 2 has 2 rows of bricks and level 3 has 3 rows of bricks. If your paddle misses the ball's rebound, you will lose a life. You have three lives to try to clear all the bricks.
